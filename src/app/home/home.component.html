Who I Am

I'm Kyle! A 3D modeler with a passion for making beautiful environments, redesigning classic characters, and mastering all things 3D. As a graduate from Full Sail University I am looking to partner with the right company to bring the creative imagination to life using tools like Autodesk Maya, Pixologic ZBrush, Allegorithmic Substance Designer/Painter, Unity3D, Epic's Unreal Engine 4, Marmoset, and Adobe Photoshop to just name a few. I want to bring new adventures to audiences all over the world. Lets make it happen!
